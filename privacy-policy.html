1. Who We Are

Amigos Plumbing & Drain Cleaning Inc ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is a licensed plumbing contractor based in Sacramento, California. We operate the website amigosplumbinganddrain.com (the "Site") to provide information about our services and allow customers to request estimates.

We are the data controller for the personal information collected through this Site. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the information in Section 15.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you fill out a contact or quote request form on our Site, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Service address or general location
  • Description of your plumbing issue or service request
  • SMS/text message consent status

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, certain technical information is collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URL (the page you came from)
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Geographic region (city/state level, derived from IP)

This data is collected via Google Analytics and the Meta Pixel, described in Sections 3 and 4 below.

3. Google Analytics & Tracking

Our Site uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4), a web analytics service provided by Google LLC ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and report information about how visitors use our Site.

What Google Analytics collects: Session duration, pages visited, traffic sources, device and browser data, geographic region, and user behavior patterns. This data is aggregated and used to understand how our website performs — not to identify you personally.

How Google Uses This Data

Google may use the data collected to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. You can review Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

Opting Out of Google Analytics

You can prevent Google Analytics from collecting your data by:

  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on
  • Enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings (note: not all sites honor this)
  • Adjusting your ad personalization settings at myaccount.google.com

4. Meta (Facebook) Pixel

Our Site uses the Meta Pixel (formerly Facebook Pixel), a tracking tool provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. ("Meta"). The Meta Pixel is a small piece of code that allows us to measure the effectiveness of our advertising by understanding the actions people take on our Site.

What the Meta Pixel collects: Page views, button clicks (such as "Book Online" or "Call Now"), form submissions, and similar user interactions. This data is sent to Meta and may be associated with your Facebook or Instagram profile if you are logged in.

How We Use Meta Pixel Data

  • To measure ad campaign performance (e.g., how many visitors came from a Facebook ad)
  • To create custom audiences for retargeting (showing our ads to people who have visited our Site)
  • To build "lookalike audiences" of people similar to our existing customers
  • To optimize our ad delivery to reach people likely to be interested in our services

Important: Meta may combine the information collected through the Pixel with other data Meta holds about you (including your Facebook profile, activity on and off Facebook, and information from other advertisers). We do not control how Meta uses this data. Please review Meta's Data Policy at facebook.com/privacy/policy.

Opting Out of Meta Tracking

  • Visit Facebook Ad Preferences to control ad personalization
  • Use the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool
  • Install an ad blocker or browser privacy extension that blocks tracking pixels
  • Enable your browser's "Do Not Track" setting (honored on a best-effort basis)

5. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to operate our Site, analyze traffic, and support our advertising efforts.

Cookie Type Provider Purpose Duration
Analytics Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat) Track site usage, sessions, and traffic sources Up to 2 years
Advertising / Pixel Meta (_fbp, _fbc) Track conversions, enable ad retargeting on Facebook/Instagram Up to 90 days
Functional Us Remember language preference and session state Session

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse new cookies, delete existing cookies, or set preferences for specific sites. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our Site. For guidance, visit your browser's help documentation or allaboutcookies.org.

6. SMS / Text Message Communications

If you provide your phone number and check the SMS consent box on our contact form, you agree to receive text messages from Amigos Plumbing & Drain Cleaning Inc. These messages may include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Service follow-ups
  • Responses to your quote requests
  • Informational messages about your service

Consent is not a condition of service. You are not required to consent to SMS communications to receive plumbing services from us. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us.

Opting Out of SMS

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message we send. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed. To re-subscribe, reply START. For help, reply HELP or contact us directly at jesse@amigosplumbinganddrain.com.

We do not share your phone number with third parties for their marketing purposes. SMS opt-in data is never shared with affiliates or other companies for marketing purposes.

7. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your service requests and inquiries — including scheduling estimates and appointments
  • To communicate with you — via phone, email, or SMS about your service request, appointment, or follow-up
  • To improve our website — using analytics data to understand which pages are most useful and how visitors navigate the Site
  • To measure and optimize advertising — using the Meta Pixel to understand which ads lead to form submissions or calls, so we can advertise more efficiently
  • To comply with legal obligations — retaining records as required by California law
  • To protect our business — detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorized access, or other harmful activity

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information to build consumer profiles for unrelated third-party marketing.

8. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

  • Contact form submissions: Retained for up to 3 years for business and tax record purposes
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 14 months in Google Analytics, after which it is automatically deleted
  • Meta Pixel data: Governed by Meta's data retention policies (typically 180 days for audience matching)
  • SMS records: Retained for the duration of our business relationship and up to 2 years after

To request deletion of your personal data, see Section 10 or contact us using the information in Section 15.

10. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), grants you specific rights regarding your personal information.

Your Rights

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell about you.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to request that we correct inaccurate personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing: We do not sell your personal information. However, our use of the Meta Pixel and Google Analytics may constitute "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes under California law. You may opt out by using the methods described in Sections 3 and 4.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights. We will not deny you services, charge you different prices, or provide a different quality of service because you exercised your privacy rights.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: We do not collect sensitive personal information (as defined by CPRA) beyond what is necessary to provide our services.

How to Exercise Your California Rights

To submit a verifiable consumer request, contact us by:

  • Phone: (916) 598-3162

We will respond to your verifiable request within 45 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request. You may designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf.

Categories of personal information collected in the past 12 months: Identifiers (name, email, phone, IP address); commercial information (services requested); internet/electronic activity (pages visited, time on site); geolocation data (city/region level); and inferences drawn from the above to understand customer preferences.
