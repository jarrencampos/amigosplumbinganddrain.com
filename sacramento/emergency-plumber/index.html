If you're dealing with any of the situations below, don't wait — call us now. Every minute of delay can mean more water damage and higher repair costs.

Burst Pipes

A burst pipe can cause major water damage to your home and belongings within minutes. Shut off your main water supply immediately, then call us. We'll have a plumber at your door fast to stop the damage and make the repair.

Call (916) 598-3162

Leaking Pipes

What starts as a small drip can quickly become a flood, damaging your floors, walls, and belongings. Don't wait and hope it stops on its own — call us before the leak becomes a much bigger, more expensive problem.

Call (916) 598-3162

Water Heater Failures

No hot water for bathing, washing dishes, or laundry is a serious disruption. Whether it's a failing heating element, a corroded tank, or a gas valve issue, we can diagnose and replace your water heater quickly to get your household back to normal.

Call (916) 598-3162

Sewer Line Backups

A sewer backup creates foul odors, hazardous conditions, and can push raw sewage back into your home. If multiple drains are clogging at once, toilets are gurgling, or you smell sewage near floor drains — that's a backup. Contact us right away.

Call (916) 598-3162

Overflowing Toilet

An overflowing toilet is not just a mess — it can cause water damage to floors and ceilings and create serious health risks from contaminated water. If plunging or snaking isn't solving the problem, the blockage is likely further down the line. Call us.

Call (916) 598-3162

Gas Leak — Evacuate First

Gas leaks are extremely dangerous and require immediate action. If you smell gas, evacuate everyone immediately, do not use any switches or open flames, and go outside before calling. Call 911 and your gas company first, then call us to handle the plumbing repair.
