Our Story

Built From the Ground Up by Jesse Vera

Jesse Vera didn't inherit a plumbing company. He built Amigos Plumbing & Drain Cleaning from scratch with a commitment to quality that most companies talk about but don't always deliver. Growing up in the Sacramento area, Jesse knew what it meant to work hard for a living, and he brought that same ethic into every service call from day one.

After years of experience working in the trade, Jesse started Amigos with a simple goal: to be the plumber he'd want to call himself. One that shows up on time, explains what's wrong in plain language, charges a fair price, and leaves the place cleaner than he found it.

"I started this company because I believe people deserve a plumber they can actually trust, someone who treats their home with respect and doesn't try to sell them something they don't need."

Jesse Vera, Owner & Founder

Today, Amigos serves homeowners and businesses across Sacramento and the surrounding communities. Whether it's a clogged drain at 2am or a full water heater replacement, Jesse and his team bring the same level of care and professionalism to every job.

How We Work

The Principles Behind Every Job

These aren't just words on a page. They're the standards Jesse holds himself and his team to on every single call.

1

Diagnose First, Recommend Second

We take the time to fully understand the problem before suggesting a solution. If something can be repaired, we'll tell you. If it needs replacing, we'll explain exactly why, in plain language, not plumber jargon.

2

Honesty Is the Baseline

We give you a straight answer and a fair price upfront. No bait-and-switch, no hidden fees. What we quote is what you pay.

3

Your Home Deserves Respect

We treat every home we enter as if it were our own. That means protecting your floors, cleaning up after ourselves, and making sure you're happy with the result before we leave.

4

Always Improving

Codes change. Technology evolves. We stay current on the latest methods and materials so we can offer you the safest, most efficient solutions available, not whatever was standard ten years ago.
