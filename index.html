Our Plumbing Services

Amigos Plumbing & Drain Cleaning is a full-service plumbing company providing residential and commercial plumbing solutions throughout the Sacramento area. We specialize in drain cleaning, sewer and water line repairs, water heater installation and replacement, toilet repairs and installations, gas line repair and replacement, leak detection, fixture installation, pipe repairs, repipes, and emergency plumbing services. Whether it's a clogged drain, broken sewer line, water heater issue, or complete plumbing system upgrade, we've got you covered.

24/7 Emergency Plumbing

Burst pipes, major leaks, gas line emergencies, sewer backups - we're always on call. Day or night, weekends and holidays, we respond fast when it matters most.

Drain & Sewer Services

From simple clog removal and routine drain cleaning to advanced hydro-jetting, camera sewer inspections, and trenchless sewer repair - we keep things flowing.

Water Heater Services

Repair, replacement, and maintenance for both traditional tank and modern tankless water heaters. We restore hot water quickly and help you choose the most efficient solution.

Leak Detection & Repair

Using advanced detection technology, we locate and fix hidden leaks in walls, concrete slabs, and underground pipes before they cause costly structural damage.

Fixture Installation & Repair

Expert service for all plumbing fixtures - faucets, toilets, sinks, showers, and garbage disposals. We install, replace, and repair fixtures to code, fast.

Maintenance & Inspections

Stay ahead of problems with thorough system evaluations and preventative maintenance plans. We identify potential issues before they become expensive repairs.
